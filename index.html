History

  • 2017 – 2018

    Two Birds in One Stone

    Clarrence Alberto and Claude Anuran were colleagues and part-timers to a small printing shop of local newspapers. The two have been friends since high school and share the same enthusiasm when it comes to writing books. Both of them had yet published works despite their skills in managing printed papers.

  • 2019

    National Writing Convention

    The 2019 National Writing Convention was a big event. It is usually held annually, and only those with invitations could attend. Alberto and Anuran were one of the luckiest pairs to get invited. In the convention were famous authors alike, some of them were even professionals. The National Writing Convention was held to commemorate authors, with or without official published works.

  • February 2019

    The Five Align

    During the National Writing Convention, Albedo and Anuran met Elles, Bagunas, and Nillusguin, who shared their own passion and enthusiasm in writing. However, they all had the same similarity; unpublished works. No company was ever taking their works. The five of them, with the knowledge of publishing books, decided to have their own publishing company.

  • January 2020

    An Organization is Born

    Bagunas was their financial support, being the real estate agent. The five writers have successfully filed their business, and is located to one of Bagunas’ own buildings.

Details

Archive Book Publishing House, Inc. prioritizes the wellness of the writers, providing a sustainable environment for their works to be published, be popularized, and be recognized.

Archive Book Publishing House, Inc. gives privilege to the green authors, as well as providing standard quality books for the readers.

The spotlight is given to the writers, keeping their thoughts, publishing and providing eligible works to the common public.

Archive Book Publishing House, Inc. works with principles and will provide the best not just for the writers, but also to the readers.
